# COS™ Ethics & Compliance Statement

The COS™ Blockchain Tracker is built on the principle of ethics-first governance. It ensures that compliance, oversight, and sustainability are not just technical outputs, but cultural commitments.

## Data Protection
- Sensitive data is never stored on-chain.
- Only SHA-256 hashes are committed to the blockchain.
- Off-chain repositories are secured with encryption and role-based access.

## Transparency & Accountability
- All governance events are immutably logged with verifiable Tx IDs.
- Oversight activities are traceable and auditable.
- ESG indicators are aggregated into a public-facing Sustainability Index.

## Community Assurance
- The system assures communities of ethical construction practices.
- It supports climate resilience, social equity, and long-term stewardship.
