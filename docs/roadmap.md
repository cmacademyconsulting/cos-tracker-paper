# COS™ Blockchain Tracker — Development Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the phased development of the COS™ Blockchain Tracker, from prototype to global certification ecosystem.

## Phase 1: Hackathon Prototype (Nov 2025)
- Finalize working paper and audit tracker
- Submit governance events on Westend testnet
- Validate Tx IDs and dashboard integration
- Prepare manifesto poster and training presentation

## Phase 2: Pilot Deployment (2026)
- Partner with donor agency for real-world implementation
- Log compliance events on mainnet or private chain
- Collect ESG metrics and stakeholder feedback
- Refine dashboard modules and reporting tools

## Phase 3: CM Academy Training Modules
- Develop instructor-led and self-paced courses
- Include blockchain logging, ESG reporting, and COS™ methodology
- Certify project managers, auditors, and donor liaisons

## Phase 4: Global Certification Ecosystem
- Launch SaaS dashboard for institutional partners
- Integrate verifiable credentials and cross-chain support
- Establish COS™ as a recognized governance standard
- Publish open-source toolkit and licensing framework
